Introduction By Ayush Suthar
    The BMW 440i Gran Coupe is a thrilling blend of performance and practicality, boasting a potent turbocharged
    6-cylinder engine and a spacious four-door layout. This particular 440i is especially exciting thanks to the M
    Performance Power and Sound Kit that increases power output to 355 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This
    440i also touts the desirable M Sport and Track Handling Packages, which add further to the driving dynamics.
    The Coral Red upholstery is a standout feature, too, providing a striking contrast to the exterior - and the 
    Harman Kardon sound system ensures a premium audio experience. This Arizona-owned 440i is a fantastic 
    example of a performance-oriented luxury car that doesn't compromise on comfort or style.
    Under the hood, this 330Ci is powered by a robust 3.0-liter straight-6 engine, delivering an impressive 225
    horsepower and 214 lb-ft of torque. Power is channeled to the rear wheels through a thrilling 6-speed manual
    transmission, enhancing the driving experience.
